About Me

I have always loved taking on challenges. The first one that shaped my professional life was when I decided to go to the College of Engineering. During my studies in college, I was introduced to the tech world, and I fell in love with it almost immediately. What I love about technology is its "Superpower" of solving almost any problem, while at the same time being able to make life easier for its users. I wanted to be part of this exciting industry.

I'm curious about any aspect of a tech business (I'm the type of guy who always asks questions), and I believe that everything is achievable if you put the right commitment into it. I learned this through my career, of nearly 8 years in web development with a variety of companies from small startups to big communities. This has made me intimately familiar with all the stages of the web lifecycle from the initial architecture to final deployment. The pace has been extremely fast, I enjoyed every minute of it.

Currently, I can't wait to see where this road takes me and I wouldn't have it any other way! Happy to connect and chat about anything tech-ecom related, so feel free to reach out.
